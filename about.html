Get to know the designer:

Victoria is a design-oriented developer. She graduated from The Fashion Institute of Technology with a degree in Photography. Victoria has spent the last 15 years working as a professional photographer. She is proficient in Lightroom and Photoshop. Victoria is currently working towards becoming a Full-Stack Web Developer.

When not working Victoria can be found curled up with a good book, snuggling with the dogs, reviewing books, testing out new soap recipes, or spending quality time with her family.
